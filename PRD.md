# ðŸ§© FEATURE REQUIREMENTS

---

## 1. Unified Job Feed

**Description**
Display job listings from a preloaded dataset in a single, clean dashboard.

**User Value**
Users don't need to browse multiple platforms.

**Requirements**

* Load jobs from dataset
* Support filtering and ranking
* No duplicates

---

## 2. Semantic Job Matching

**Description**
Match jobs to users based on meaning, not keywords.

**User Value**
Users see jobs that actually fit their skills and intent.

**Requirements**

* Resume/profile embedding
* Job description embedding
* Similarity-based ranking

---

## 3. Job Fit Score

**Description**
Show a percentage score representing how well a job matches the user.

**User Value**
Instant understanding of suitability.

**Requirements**

* Skill match calculation
* Experience alignment
* Location and preference checks

---

## 4. Explainable Match Breakdown

**Description**
Explain *why* a job fits or doesn't fit.

**User Value**
Builds trust and helps users improve.

**Requirements**

* Matched skills
* Missing skills
* Risk factors

---

## 5. "Should I Apply?" Decision Engine

**Description**
AI explicitly recommends **Apply / Wait / Skip / Avoid**.

**User Value**
Prevents wasted applications.

**Requirements**

* Fit score thresholds
* Competition estimation
* Career alignment check

---

## 6. Career Risk Detection

**Description**
Warn users if a job may harm long-term career growth.

**User Value**
Protects career trajectory.

**Requirements**

* Detect repetitive roles
* Low skill growth indicators
* Misalignment with goals

---

## 7. Ghost & Low-Quality Job Detection

**Description**
Identify jobs unlikely to result in real hiring.

**User Value**
Saves time and reduces frustration.

**Requirements**

* Posting age analysis
* Description clarity scoring
* Duplicate repost detection

---

## 8. Competition Level Estimation

**Description**
Estimate how competitive a job is.

**User Value**
Helps users choose realistic opportunities.

**Requirements**

* Similar applicant density (simulated)
* Skill saturation logic

---

## 9. Alternative Role Suggestions

**Description**
Suggest better-fit roles users may not be searching for.

**User Value**
Expands career opportunities intelligently.

**Requirements**

* Skill adjacency analysis
* Role similarity detection

---

## 10. Skill Gap & Learning Roadmap

**Description**
Show missing skills and how to close gaps.

**User Value**
Turns rejection into progress.

**Requirements**

* Skill gap detection
* Short learning plan generation

---

## 11. Job Comparison View

**Description**
Allow side-by-side comparison of similar roles.

**User Value**
Improves decision-making.

**Requirements**

* Compare fit, risk, growth
* Highlight recommended choice

---

## 12. Smart Notifications (Optional)

**Description**
Notify users only when high-value opportunities appear.

**User Value**
Reduces noise and anxiety.

**Requirements**

* Fit-based alerts
* User control
